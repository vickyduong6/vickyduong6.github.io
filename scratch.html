Description of the game

A game where it tests your knowledge and wit of the community of La Quinta High School.

Describe some programming concepts or structures you learned in this unit that you applied in your game.

We have learned te concept of algorithems which are step by step procedures that gives directions on what our sprite has to do. We also used if-else statements to describe if an action doesn't occur, this must happen

What aspects of your game did you like best?

I liked how it related to everyone in the class, and how one needed to be clever in order to answer the questions or solve the riddles.

If more time was given for you to work on your game, what would you improve or modify about your game?

I would of added more "mini games" to increase the user's entertainment. I also would of incorporated more lives so people have the chance to see all the questions; a lot of people for frustrated with having 10 questions but only having 2 lives.
